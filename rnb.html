Spicy Red Rice and Beans

Not Yo Mamma's Rice N Beans

When I take a spoonful of this delicious dish, I instanly picture myself on a beach in Puerto Rico! it's sure to spice up your life!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb brown rice
  • 3 cans of red pinto beans
  • small red chilis
  • red pepper flakes
  • paprika

Steps

  1. boil brown rice for 45 min until tender
  2. wash red pinto beans and simmer on stove until hot
  3. when rice is done, let cool and then plate
  4. mix the paprika and red pepper flakes with the pinto beans
  5. spoon the beans and sauce over the rice and enjoy!
