Lasagna Recipe

Marc's Famous Lasagna

This recipe brings back memories of my childhood. My mom used to make this on cold days for the entire family. It will fill your house with pleasant aromas and fill your belly!!

Ingredients

  • lasagna pasta
  • tomato sauce
  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • parmesean cheese
  • mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. coat your baking pan with butter
  3. bake lasagna pasta as directed and place in baking dish
  4. add cooked ground beef, shredded mozz, and ricotta cheese to baking dish
  5. bake for 45 min until top is golden brown
  6. let cool for 10 min, serve, and ENJOY!!
