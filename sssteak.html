Salisbury Steak

Marc's Salivating Salisbury Steak

This is a warm and hearty dish. The flavor will explode in your mouth and you'll be sure to tell your friends!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of skirt steak
  • steak seasoning
  • bag of potatoes
  • parmesean cheese
  • mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. coat your baking pan with butter
  3. peel and boil the potatoes. mash and add milk-butter until a good consistency
  4. make gravy on the side and pour over mashed potatoes
  5. plate your mashed potatoes and gravy and mix in the skirt steak
  6. let cool for 10 min, serve, and ENJOY!!
